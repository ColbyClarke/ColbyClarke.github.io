Neighbouring the Lion and Safari Park and situated within a nature reserve area, Kwa n'Jala has an out of Africa rustic theme with a touch of comfort. You should hear the lions roar as well as the drums from Lesedi Cultural Village in the evening.

Kwa n'Jala is situated between Lanseria Airport and Hartbeesport Dam.

The cottage is self-catering and has braai facilities.

Guests can stroll across to the Lion and Safari Park and have access to the restaurant and shopping amenities. We are situated over the road from Lesedi Cultural Village and are within close driving distance to various restaurants and Hartbeesport Dam.
