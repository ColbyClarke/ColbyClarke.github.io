Things to do around Lanseria


Cradle of Humankind

Scientists believe that the origin of modern-day humans rests here. Declared a World Heritage Site by UNESCO in 1999, the site is home to the largest concentration of human ancestral remains anywhere in the world. The site currently occupies 47,000 hectares (180 sq mi) and contains a complex system of limestone caves.

Lion Park

Get closer than you could ever imagine to Africa's most incredible wildlife. Feel the breath of the lion and the thundering of the gnu's hoofs, all from the safety of our guided game drive or your own vehicle.

Harties Cableway

Enjoy a trip on the cableway to a hilltop with amazing panoramic views of the beautiful Magaliesberg Mountains. Visitors can enjoy mountain walks or relax in the dining areas.
