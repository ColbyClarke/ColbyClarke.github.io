The space

Kwa n'Jala means Place of Nyala. It has easy access off the Lanseria Road, but you will find yourselves snugly nestled in your own small private nature area. The property lies within the Cradle of Humankind and the Farm is on the original site of the Anglo Boer Battle called the Ambush of Kalkheuvel.

Guest access

Please ask for a key and gate access if you would like to walk in the adjoining nature area. Please also ask about a discount to visit the Lion and Safari Park and for information about its activities.

Other things to note

The guest cottage is privately fenced within several secluded hectares which is then included within a larger nature conservation area. Guests may walk in the area which has several species of antelope, porcupine and jackal and are asked to respect the nature of this area.
